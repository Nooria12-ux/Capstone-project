About Me

Favourite Cartoon

Doraemon is a friendly, futuristic robot cat from the 22nd century who travels back in time to help a boy named Nobita Nobi. Shaped like a round blue cat without ears, he has a four-dimensional pocket on his belly that holds various gadgets, such as the "Anywhere Door" and "Time Machine." Despite his advanced technology, Doraemon often faces comedic mishaps due to his clumsy and kind-hearted nature. His mission is to improve Nobita's life and guide him toward a brighter future, blending humor, adventure, and heartfelt lessons in their escapades.

Hobby

Painting is a timeless art form that brings pleasure by stimulating our senses and emotions. Through colors, shapes, and textures, it captures beauty, tells stories, and evokes feelings, allowing us to experience the world from the artist's perspective. Paintings often serve as a visual escape, inspiring awe or tranquility, and can transport us to imagined landscapes or historical moments. They also invite personal interpretation, encouraging viewers to connect their own memories and feelings to the artwork. This combination of aesthetic appeal and emotional resonance makes painting a uniquely enriching and pleasurable experience.

Future

I want to become a doctor because I am deeply passionate about helping others and making a meaningful difference in people’s lives. The idea of using my skills and knowledge to alleviate pain, save lives, and promote health inspires me. I am fascinated by the complexity of the human body and the ever-evolving nature of medical science, which offers endless opportunities to learn and grow. Becoming a doctor will allow me to combine my compassion for others with a challenging and fulfilling career dedicated to improving the well-being of individuals and communities.
