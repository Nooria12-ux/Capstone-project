The Places I have visited

Jamidar Bari

Jamidar Bari in Tangail is a historical palace built by wealthy landlords during the British colonial period. It is a beautiful and grand building with tall pillars, wide verandas, and intricate designs. The palace shows the rich cultural and architectural heritage of that time. Many visitors come to see it and learn about the history of the zamindars (landowners) in Bengal. My school took us to the Jamidar Bari last year. It was beautiful and it also had a museum beside it. We had a lot of fun there.

Jamidar Bari

Shonshod Bhaban

Shonshod Bhaban, also known as the National Parliament House, is a famous building in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was designed by architect Louis Kahn and is one of the largest legislative buildings in the world. The building is made of concrete and has a unique design with geometric shapes and large openings that let in natural light. It is surrounded by a beautiful lake and gardens, making it a popular spot for visitors. It was very big yet so beautiful. Beside the Shonshod Bhaban, there was a big flower garden and the place would shine due to the lighting systems.

Shonshod Bhaban

Padma River

The Padma River is one of the major rivers in Bangladesh. It is a large and important waterway, flowing from the Ganges River in India and joining with other rivers in Bangladesh. I went to see the Padma River, its refreshing view made me pleased. Its very big and breathtakingly beautiful.
